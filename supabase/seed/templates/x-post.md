# X Post Template

## Frontmatter
title, created, platform: x, status: draft, source, reader, pain, belief, point, meaning, proof

## Body structure
- Line 1: Hook — stops the scroll, one clear idea
- Line 2: Tension or contradiction
- Lines 3-5: Body with proof
- Final line: Ask, punchline, or question

## Rules
- Max 280 characters total
- One idea only
- Every line on its own
- No em dashes, no engagement bait
- Strong first 5 seconds — can a stranger get the idea in one glance?

## Rhythm
- One blank line between every sentence
- Short lines. One thought per line.
- End with a specific number, question, or reframe
