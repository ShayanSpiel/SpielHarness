# LinkedIn Post Template

## Frontmatter
title, created, platform: linkedin, status: draft, source, reader, pain, belief, point, meaning, proof

## Body structure
- Hook paragraph: sets up the reader's situation or pain
- Narrative arc: belief → contradiction → proof → meaning
- Proof section: 2-3 short evidence points with specifics
- Meaning: the aha, first-person ICP voice
- CTA: question or invitation to engage

## Rules
- Max 3000 characters
- Scannable: short paragraphs with blank lines between them
- One idea per section
- End with a question or invitation, not a pitch
- No engagement bait or corporate language

## Rhythm
- Paragraphs of 1-3 sentences each
- Blank line between paragraphs
- Specific proof with numbers where possible
