# Email Newsletter Template

## Frontmatter
title, created, platform: email, format: newsletter, status: draft, source, reader, pain, belief, point, meaning, proof

## Body structure
- Subject line: 40-60 chars, triggers curiosity
- Greeting: personal, warm
- Hook: the situation or insight
- Body: narrative with proof — what changed and why it matters
- Meaning: what this means for the reader
- CTA: one clear action or reflection
- Sign-off

## Rules
- Max 5000 characters
- Scannable: short paragraphs, bold key phrases, bullet points where helpful
- One main idea per newsletter
- No pitch — deliver value first, CTA last
- Plain text style — feels like a personal email, not a marketing blast
