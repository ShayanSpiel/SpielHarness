# Blog Post Template

## Frontmatter
title, created, platform: blog, status: draft, source, reader, pain, belief, point, meaning, proof

## Body structure
- Hook / lede: drop the reader into the problem
- Context: the old belief — what everyone thinks
- The contradiction: the new point — what's actually true
- Proof: 3 concrete examples or data points with analysis
- Meaning: what this means for the reader
- CTA: what to do next

## Rules
- Max 2500 words
- Sections with subheadings
- Proof with numbers and specifics
- No fluff paragraphs — every sentence moves the argument forward
- End with forward motion, not summary

## Rhythm
- Subheadings break up sections
- Short paragraphs (2-4 sentences)
- One example per proof point
- Closing is the call to action, not "in conclusion"
