# Email Sequence Template

## Frontmatter
title, created, platform: email, format: sequence, status: draft, source, reader, pain, belief, point, meaning, proof

## Structure (3-email sequence)

### Email 1: The Hook
- Subject: triggers the pain or curiosity
- Body: name the pattern the reader lives in
- End: "In the next email, I'll show you the shift."

### Email 2: The Contradiction
- Subject: reframes the belief
- Body: the point + proof
- End: "Tomorrow: what this means for you."

### Email 3: The Meaning + CTA
- Subject: the aha
- Body: what changes now + one clear action
- CTA: specific, low-friction next step

## Rules
- Each email: max 3000 chars
- Self-contained — reader should learn something even if they skip an email
- No hard sell — value first, invitation second
- Same voice across all 3 emails
- Space 24-48 hours between sends
