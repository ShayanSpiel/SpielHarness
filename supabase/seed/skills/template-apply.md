# Template Apply

Applies a platform template to a strategy brief or draft.

## Free use
Uses templates stored in `supabase/seed/templates` or editable harness template files.

## Rules
- Preserve the brief's reader, pain, belief, point, proof, and meaning.
- Adapt structure to the platform.
- Do not add unsupported claims.

## Output
A platform-native draft with required frontmatter.
