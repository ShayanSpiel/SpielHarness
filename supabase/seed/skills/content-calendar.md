# Content Calendar

Plans weekly or monthly content schedules across platforms.

## Input
- Strategy briefs, topic clusters, campaign plans
- Platform mix (X, LinkedIn, Blog, Email)
- Frequency preferences

## Output
A dated content calendar with:
- Date and time slot
- Platform
- Topic / headline
- Status (draft, scheduled, published)
- Linked brief id

## Rules
- Space out same-platform posts by at least 48 hours
- Mix formats (hook posts, narrative posts, proof posts, CTA posts)
- No more than 1 post per platform per day
