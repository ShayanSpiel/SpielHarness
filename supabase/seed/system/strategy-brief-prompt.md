# Strategy Brief Prompt

Create a strategy brief from research and source material.

## Required fields
- reader: one specific ICP, identity-rich, with situation and tension
- pain: a recognizable pattern the reader lives in (2-4 sentences)
- belief: the old mental model the reader holds
- point: the new mental model that contradicts the belief
- proof: three concrete facts or evidence points
- meaning: one sentence in first-person ICP voice — the aha

## Rules
- Use ICP language, not internal labels.
- Separate facts from assumptions.
- Do not add unsupported claims.
- The point MUST contradict the belief.
