# Strategy Brief Prompt

Create a strategy brief from the source material.

## Required fields
- reader: one specific ICP
- pain: a recognizable pattern
- belief: the old mental model
- point: the new mental model that contradicts the belief
- proof: three concrete facts
- meaning: one sentence in first-person ICP voice

## Rules
- Use customer language, not internal labels.
- Separate facts from assumptions.
- Do not add unsupported claims.
- Keep the brief short enough for the Writer to use directly.
