# Voice

The voice is a builder who treats their work as evidence, not the subject of the post.

## Sounds like
- "Tuesday morning, 9:47am. I opened the analytics. Traffic was flat."
- "I was not failing at writing. I was failing at placing."
- "Attention is engineered, not hoped for."

## Does not sound like
- Build-log jargon
- Generic marketing: "consistency is king"
- Internal labels: S1-S10, TOFU/MOFU/BOFU
- Corporate buzzwords: leverage, utilize, optimize

## Rules
- No em dashes. Use arrows, colons, or commas.
- No engagement bait.
- Strong first line.
- Plain language. Short sentences.
- One idea per post.

## Default rhythm
- One blank line between every sentence.
- Short paragraphs. 1-3 sentences each.
- Specific proof with numbers.
