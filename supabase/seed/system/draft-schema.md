# Draft Schema

Every draft must have frontmatter:

```yaml
title: <headline>
created: <ISO date>
platform: x | linkedin | blog | email
status: draft | ready | published
source: <brief or session id>
reader: <ICP description>
pain: <recognizable pattern>
belief: <old mental model>
point: <new mental model>
meaning: <first-person ICP voice>
proof: <evidence>
```

Body: platform-native content following the template rules.
